About Us

Welcome to Fishly, your trusted source for the finest GIFT (Genetically Improved Farmed Tilapia) in the heart of Teekoy village, Kottayam district. At Fishly, we are dedicated to sustainable aquaculture practices that not only provide you with delicious and nutritious seafood but also contribute to the preservation of our environment. Founded in 2018, Fishly has grown from a humble family-owned farm into a thriving aquaculture enterprise. Our passion for fish farming and a commitment to delivering the highest quality GIFT Tilapia to our customers have been the driving forces behind our success. Our Farm is located on Kandathinkara, Aniyilappu, Teekoy, our state-of-the-art fish farm spans over 5 Acres and boasts three meticulously maintained ponds where our GIFT Tilapia thrive. These ponds are not just places of cultivation but also ecosystems where we prioritize the well-being of our fish and the surrounding environment.

Our Specialties

Premium GIFT Tilapia

Our core specialty is the production of GIFT (Genetically Improved Farmed Tilapia), a remarkable fish variety known for its superior taste and nutritional value. We raise GIFT Tilapia with care and precision, ensuring they meet the highest quality standards.

Sustainable Farming

Sustainability is at the heart of what we do. We employ eco-friendly farming practices, including responsible feed management, efficient water usage, and ethical treatment of our fish. When you choose Fishly, you support environmentally conscious aquaculture.

Customized Orders

We understand that our customers have unique needs. That's why we offer customized order options to accommodate your specific requirements, whether you need fillets, whole fish, or special packaging.

Retail and Wholesale

Whether you're a chef, restaurant owner, or retailer, we cater to both retail and wholesale customers. Our commitment to quality remains unwavering, no matter the scale of your order.
