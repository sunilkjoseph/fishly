About Us

Welcome to Fish.ly, where the art of aquaculture meets cutting-edge sustainability practices. Our fish farm, spanning an expansive 5 acres in the picturesque setting of Kandathinkara, Aniyilappu, Teekoy, Kottayam, is a testament to our unwavering commitment to redefining seafood production.

Innovative Biofloc Technology:

At Fish.ly, we pride ourselves on pioneering sustainable aquaculture with our innovative Biofloc technology. In this environmentally friendly process, waste products are transformed by beneficial microorganisms right within our ponds. This remarkable ecosystem breakdown provides an organic food supply for our Tilapia, ensuring they thrive naturally.

Delicious and Responsible Seafood:

Our GIFT Tilapia, raised through the Biofloc method, are not only delicious but also environmentally responsible. By utilizing this innovative approach, we guarantee that our fish are free from harmful chemicals and preservatives, allowing you to enjoy the pure, natural taste of Tilapia at its finest.

MACROS

GIFT (Genetically Improved Farmed Tilapia) Tilapia offers a nutritious and health-conscious choice for those seeking a lean source of protein. In a 100-gram serving, GIFT Tilapia provides approximately 96 calories, which is notably lower than the 165 calories found in an equivalent serving of chicken breast. Moreover, GIFT Tilapia boasts a substantial protein content, with around 21 grams per 100 grams, making it an excellent option for those looking to meet their protein needs while keeping their calorie intake in check.

Even more impressive is the amount of vitamins and minerals in this fish. This fish offers essential vitamins and minerals, including vitamin B12, niacin, phosphorus, selenium, and vitamin D, contributing to a balanced and nutrient-rich diet. With minimal saturated fat and no carbohydrates or sugars, GIFT Tilapia is a smart choice for individuals focused on healthy eating and wholesome nutrition.
